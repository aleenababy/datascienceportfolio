I developed a chemical network tool capable of parsing ASCII files to extract chemical reactions from the model. It transforms these reactions into reactants, products, and nodes. The pipeline is designed to process hdf5 output from the KOSMA-tau PDR model. The ultimate objective is to generate a comprehensive chemical network that links the species formed and destroyed through various chemical reactions.
